Placki ziemniaczane (Polish Potato Pancakes)

Description

Polish potato pancakes, known as placki kartoflane or placki ziemniaczane in Polish, are delicious and delightful. This traditional recipe comes from Gwizda≈Çy village in the Mazovia region of Poland and it's surprisingly easy.

Ingredients:

  • 6 medium potatoes, peeled and finely grated (about 2 1/4 pounds total)
  • 1 medium onion, finely grated
  • 2 large eggs
  • Kosher salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour, more as needed
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Granulated sugar, for garnish
  • Sour cream, for garnish
  • Applesauce, for garnish
